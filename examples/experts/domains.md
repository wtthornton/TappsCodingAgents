# Project Domains

This file defines the business domains for your project and their primary experts.

## Project: Example Multi-Domain System

This project involves multiple business domains, each with a dedicated expert.

### Domain 1: Home Automation

- **Primary Expert**: expert-home-automation
- **Description**: Smart home systems, IoT device management, automation rules, user experience for smart homes
- **Key Areas**: Device protocols (Zigbee, Z-Wave, Matter), automation triggers, energy management integration

### Domain 2: Healthcare

- **Primary Expert**: expert-healthcare
- **Description**: Healthcare applications, HIPAA compliance, medical data handling, patient privacy
- **Key Areas**: PHI protection, audit trails, regulatory compliance, interoperability standards

### Domain 3: Financial Services

- **Primary Expert**: expert-financial-services
- **Description**: Fintech applications, banking systems, payment processing, financial regulations
- **Key Areas**: PCI DSS compliance, fraud detection, transaction security, regulatory reporting

### Domain 4: E-commerce

- **Primary Expert**: expert-ecommerce
- **Description**: Online retail platforms, payment gateways, inventory management, customer experience
- **Key Areas**: Shopping cart flows, payment integration, order fulfillment, product catalogs
